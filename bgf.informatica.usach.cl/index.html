Introducing BGF

BGames is a software and a guide, aimed at gamedevelopers, observational researchers, that facilitates work with multimodal data and combinar realidad con virtual.

  • Multimodal

    Allows unification of various datasets obtained from different sources.

  • Extensible

    Any developer can create extensions to provide support for new sources, data types and even add new modules.

  • Multiplatform

    Developed with Java, capable of running in different operative systems.

  • Open Source

    Anyone can obtain BGF free of charge. We encourage the community to extend it’s functionalities.

BGF purpose

BGF was developed at Departamento de Ingeniería Informática from Universidad de Santiago de Chile.
